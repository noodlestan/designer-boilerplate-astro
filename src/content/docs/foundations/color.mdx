## Color Palettes

Our color palettes extend our core brand colors, providing a range of shades that maintain consistency and flexibility in design. These palettes are specifically tuned for different use cases while preserving the visual identity.

### Pink and Blue Palettes

The band color Palettes provide a broad range of tints and shades of our brand color. It is designed for use in icons and backgrounds but should not be used for typography.

The shades follow a controlled progression, adjusting lightness and saturation channels in the [Oklch color space](https://oklch.com/#61.92,0.1717,358.75,100) to maintain balance across the palette.

### Heat Map Color Set

The Heat Map Color Set is a structured 7-step gradient designed for data visualization. Spanning from warm to cool tones, it provides a clear and effective way to represent intensity variations in heat maps. This palette is not meant for typography, icons, backgrounds, or foregrounds.
